Fellowship Interview Coach

Structured, medicine-specific preparation tool for residents

Profile

1. Tell Your Story

Coach Prompts:
• What experiences pulled you toward this specialty?
• What patient population or problem keeps you engaged?
• What kind of physician do you hope to become?
• Think about a rotation, mentor, or patient
• Focus on specifics, not general statements

2. Program Fit

Coach Prompts:
• What specifically draws you to this program?
• Which faculty or clinical opportunities matter most?
• What thoughtful questions will help you decide fit?

3. Evidence Bank

Coach Prompts:
• What stories demonstrate your growth?
• Which case shows clinical reasoning or compassion?
• How have you handled challenge or conflict?

4. Practice

Coach Prompts:
• Are you answering clearly and directly?
• Are you using specific examples?
• Where can you be more concise?

5. Logistics

Coach Prompts:
• Is your environment distraction-free?
• Is your tech tested and reliable?
• How will you capture impressions after the interview?
